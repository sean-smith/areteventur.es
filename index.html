About the Founder

Alex Abate

Hi, I'm Alex Abate — founder of Arête Ventures, and a firm believer that work should be both bold and deeply human.

With a Bachelor's in Mechanical Engineering and a Master's in Industrial‑Organizational Psychology, I live at the intersection of systems thinking, purpose‑driven strategy, and leadership development.

Over the past decade, I've supported organizations across construction, retail, consulting, and real‑estate development — helping teams streamline complexity, lead with integrity, and create work that matters.

My approach is shaped by a simple truth: people are designed to create. When we align our structure with our values, the work becomes not just successful — but deeply satisfying.
